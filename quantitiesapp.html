    Our Story

    Every groundbreaking App begins with a series of small ideas that are combined into an all inclusive one. At Quantities , we wanted to change how data is entered into a spreadsheet. We wanted to remove numerous time consuming steps required to tabulate data and to share data. This is where the Quantities app comes in. It is perfect for when you are collecting a few rows to a few hundred rows of data.
